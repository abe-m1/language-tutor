HOW IT WORKS

Build your tribe

Trulo pulls from your contacts and their contacts to generate a network of true and trusted locals around the globe.

Pick your city

Wherever you are traveling next, we’ll connect you with trulos in your desired destination who have the local knowledge to help you find your hidden travel gems.

Give a lil' get a lil'

Select what you’re up for: Hosting, grabbing a bite, or being an emergency contact. Upload your best pic of your current city and list favorite spots to dine, drink and play.

TRIBE OF 5

Once invited by a trulo user, build your tribe.

Invite five like-minded travel friends to keep our network trusted and authentic.
Connect with their inner circles and uncover the hidden gems, not the tourist traps.
